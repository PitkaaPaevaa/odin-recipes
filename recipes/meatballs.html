Swedish meatballs

This Swedish meatball recipe is our family's Christmas tradition. We frequently double the recipe and keep it warm in a slow cooker. Worth the effort, and the meatballs are even better the next day! Reserve brown gravy and add sour cream to it the day you serve the leftovers.

  • 2 slices day-old white bread, crumbled
  • half cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • small onion, minced
  • pound ground beef
  • one third of pound finely ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Place bread crumbs into small bowl
  3. Meanwhile melt 1 spoon butter in skiller over medium heat
  4. mix ground beef, fround pork, egg, brown sugar, salt, black pepper, nutmeg, allspice and ginger with onion in the mixing bowl
