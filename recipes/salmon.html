Cedar Planked Salmon

  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions
  • Fresh ginger
  • Minced garlic
  1. Soak cedar plank for 1 hour
  2. Stir soy sauce with rest of the indegredients
  3. Place Salmon in soy mixture
  4. Preheat grill for medium heat
  5. Remove salmon from marinade
  6. Grill salmon
